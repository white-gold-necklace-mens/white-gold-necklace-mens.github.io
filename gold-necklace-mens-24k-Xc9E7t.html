The Gold Standard: Composition and Purity

14K gold jewelry is not pure gold, but rather a carefully crafted alloy. It consists of 58.3% pure gold, meaning 14 parts out of 24 are pure gold. The remaining 41.7% is composed of various alloy metals like silver, copper, and sometimes palladium. This specific ratio is what gives 14K gold its remarkable properties.

This blend provides an ideal balance of gold's inherent value and its practical resistance to daily wear.
